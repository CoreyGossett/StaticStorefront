Who Is Smart Boy Studios?

Smart Boy Studios is a local label and music collective based out of Dayton, Ohio. Founded by, Jordan Murphy aka Ash Matthews, and comprised of Corey Gossett, Dorian "Dcasso" Bradford, Cameron "C Money" Grey, Derrick "Dswove" Swoveland, Jacob "Vesta" Sturgill, Alex "Zander" Hankins. We work hard to bring solid entertainment services to the local public.
